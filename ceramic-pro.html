Ceramic Pro

About the Product

What is Ceramic Pro?

Ceramic Pro is a multi-layerable, clear, liquid nano-ceramic coating. When cured, this technology transforms itself on the surface to become a rigid, flexible glass shield. Ceramic Pro can be described as an additional clear coat, with three times the hardness and self-cleaning properties.

As a certified Ceramic Pro installer, MX Detail applies this professional-grade coating to protect your vehicle's paint, glass, wheels, and interior surfaces from environmental damage, UV rays, chemical stains, and minor scratches.

Unlike traditional waxes and sealants that wash away over time, Ceramic Pro forms a permanent bond with your vehicle's factory paint that will not wash away or break down. Each layer provides added protection and gloss.

Why Ceramic Pro

Benefits of Ceramic Pro

Hydrophobic

Super hydrophobic effect causes water to bead up and roll off the surface, making your vehicle easier to clean and maintain.

UV Protection

Protects your vehicle's paint from harmful UV rays that cause oxidation, fading, and dulling over time.

Chemical Resistance

Resists acidic contaminants such as bird droppings, bug splatter, tree sap, and industrial fallout that can etch into unprotected paint.

Enhanced Gloss

Adds incredible depth and clarity to your paint, delivering a mirror-like finish that turns heads and keeps your vehicle looking showroom-new.
